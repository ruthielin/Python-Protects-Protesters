What social problem does this project address?

2020 has been the year of the unprecedented, both due to the spread of COVID-19 and the popularization of the Black Lives Matter movement. PPP, or Python Protects Protesters, combines these "super issues" and helps protestors support the cause while staying safe. Using PPP, protesters can see all the previous BLM protests in their area and also get a risk evaluation for how dangerous it is to protest in their county.

How is AI integrated into PPP?

PPP implements a supervised Machine Learning algorithm known as Linear Regression when it determines the arbitrary risk percentage. By implementing this algorithm we determined how much each of the chosen factors (heart disease death rate, poverty rate, unemployment rate, median household income, percentage of 65+ population, percentage of minorities, population density) influenced the percent of the county population infected (as of 06/21/20). Then using these weights, we accordingly developed a mathematically formula to calculate our Risk scale (1-100).
